reaction_ammo_sver

[OBJECT:REACTION]


	!! This file is part of the OPTIONAL metal rework - default metal rework files are located in the mod's main folder.
		Wrought iron, hardened bronze and tempered steel have been renamed for cleaner interface.
		Vanilla iron, steel and bronze have been renamed for distinction from modded metals.
		Base reactions now use unworked (vanilla) metals, in the same proportions as making modded metal bars does.
		This, however, allows to save up a bit of fuel (and sand, in case of iron and steel).


	The reactions to make certain ammo types more easily accessible and/or cheaper for the player.
	Optional, but recommended.
	
	Ammo from arrowhead kits is assembled at the Bowyer's Workshop by a Bowyer.
	Arrowheads kits themselves are made at the Metalsmith's Forge (or cast at the Smelter) by a Weaponsmith.
	Ammo from non-economic rock is made at the Craftsdwarf's workshop by a Stonecrafter.
	Ammo from clay is made at the Kiln by a Potter, requires fuel.
	Making lead/zinc bullet casting kits and casting sling bullets with them is done at the Metalsmith's Forge by a Furnace Operator, requires fuel.
